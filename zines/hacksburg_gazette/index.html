Hacksburg is a Makerspace in Blacksburg, VA

Building a wooden box in Hacksburg's Woodworking 101 class

From rockets to robots to rocking chairs, whatever it is, you can probably make it at Hacksburg. Our Community Workshop hosts instructional classes and weekly work sessions, aiming to help the NRV community complete projects that would otherwise be out of reach.

Hacksburg's woodshop, metalshop, laser cutter, 3D printers, electronics bench, embroidery machine, vinyl cutter, and CNC mill are available for use by Hacksburg members twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week.
